About Me

Hello and welcome to my little corner of the internet! If you are here, it is either because I forced you to look at my site or be interested in learning more about me as a developer. Either way, I appreciate you taking the time to visit me here!

A little about me and my experiences: my name is Shawn, I am originally from Tennessee, but I currently reside in Roseville, MN, with my wife, son, and our two cats. Throughout my life, I have always had a passion for the creative. From a job as a Professional Dancer with the Nashville Repertory theatre to Front-End design of Flash Mobile Applications, I have found ways to keep the creative side of my brain invested.

With my experience in crucial leadership positions such as Warehouse Manager and Artistic Director of two Stillwater Middle Schools, I bring over 20 years of hands-on, creative problem-solving experience. I am also experienced in team collaboration, project scoping with local and district leaders, and over 30 years of experience growing my communication skills.

That is me in a nutshell! Please check out my portfolio page if you would like to see some examples of my work. Thank you very much for your time! I wish you a peaceful and joyful day!
